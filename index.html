Introduction

Blue Eyes is a technology conducted by the research team of IBM at its Almaden Research Center in San Jose, California since 199. The aim of the blue eyes technology is to give human power or abilities to a computer, so that the machine can naturally interact with human beings as we interact with each other.Blue eye system provides technical means for monitoring and recording the operator's basic physiological parameters. Uses sensing technology. The most important parameter is saccadic activity ( Saccade is a rapid eye jump to a new location assigned by the conscious attention).

Blue Eyes

System Overvieww

Data Acquisition Unit (DAU)
Microcontroller- ATMEL 89C52 is heart of the DAU. Mobile component of the system. Its function is to gather physiological information from sensors and forward to CSU for processing and verification purposes.

Central System Unit (CSU)
It contains a Bluetooth module and a PCM codec for voice data transmission. It serves as Connection Management, Data processing and recording, Access verification, System Maintenance.
